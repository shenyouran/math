The Magic 0

Introduction: 0 is a magical number. Sometimes when we say 0, we mean that there's nothing. But if we say it's 0 degrees outside, it doesn't mean that it's nothing! Let's learn about the magic of 0!

Any Number Divide by 0

1. Why does any number divide by 0 get undefined?

For example, pick a number 1. And use 1 to divide by 0.

2. Next, show examples of 1 that divides nuumbers that are close to 0, but are positive numbers.

1/0.1=10

1/0.01=100

1/0.001=1,000

1/0.0001=10,000

...

1/0=positive infinite

3. Next, show examples of 1 that divides nuumbers that are close to 0, but are negative numbers.

1/-0.1=-10

1/-0.01=-100

1/-0.001=-1,000

1/-0.0001=-10,000

...

1/0=negative infinite

Because the two answers are different, so any number divide by 0 is undefined.
