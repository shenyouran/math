Two-Digit Multiplication from 10~20

Introduction: Going crazy with two-digit multiplication expressions! Let's learn another type of two-digit multiplication!

Pick integers.

Pick integers from randomly from 10~20. For example 12 and 14.

First, multiply the tens-digit. Then add the ones-digit and multiply it. Last, put them together.

(1) 1*1=1
(2) 2+4=6
(3) 2*4=8

Put 1, 6, and 8 together and get 168!
