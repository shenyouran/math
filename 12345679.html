The Magic 12,345,679

Introduction: What do you notice about 12,345,679? It has a varity of 1~9, but doesn't have an 8.

Do A Trick of Multiplying a Number That Can Be Equally Divided by 9

1. Ask a friend what his/her favorite number is, from 1~9. Prepare a calulator!

2. Use your calulator and first press 12,345,679 and the "*" button. Multiply 9 by what your friend's favorite number is.

12,345,679*9=111,111,111

12,345,679*18=222,222,222

12,345,679*27=333,333,333

12,345,679*36=444,444,444

12,345,679*45=555,555,555

And so on...until 81.

Do A Trick of Multiplying a Number That Can Be Equally Divided by 3

Prepare your calulator and multiply 12,345,679*12.

What do you get? It's 148,148,148!

Do Another Trick of Multiplying 11~19 (but not numbers that could be equally divided by 3)

Multiply 11, 13, 14, 16, 17, and 19.

What do you get? (pretend 12,345,679 is n)

11n=135,802,469 (less 7)

13n=160,493,827 (less 5)

14n=172,839,506 (less 4)

16n=197,530,864 (less 2)

17n=209,876,543 (less 1)

19n=234,567,901 (less 8)

What happens? The number you get seperately doesn't have a number from 0~9 (except 0, 3, 6, and 9)
