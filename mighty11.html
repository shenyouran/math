The Mighty 11

Introduction: 11 is a very naughty number! You know why? It's maybe because of that it is the sum of 10 and 1.

Any Number

1. Pick any number.

For example, pick a number 315.

2. Add.

Add the first two numbers, 3+1=4, and the last two numbers, 1+5=6. Then keep the first and last digit, and get 3,465.

The mighty 11 is very cool. Just add some digits and you'll get the right answer!

A Two Digit Number

Normal Two-Digit Numbers

1. Pick a two digit number.

Be careful to pick a number between 10~99. Don't "go random"!

For example, pick 45.

2. Again, add.

Add the two digits and put the sum between the two-digit number.

4+5=9, put the 9 between the 4 and 5, and get 495!

Two-Digit Numbers That Needs Carrying

1. If you pick a two-digit number like 95, then look how you solve 95 times 11.

2. Add 9+5 and put the sum between it.

9+5=14. How can you do this? Carry the 1. Carry the 1 again and get 1,045.
