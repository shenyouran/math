Black Hole Numbers

Introduction: We will learn on of the easiest black hole numbers now.

1. Pick a random number.

For example, pick a number 239.

2. Reorder.

Look at how many odd and even numbers it has, and put how many even numbers on the hundreds-place, odd numbers on the tens-place, and the sum on the ones-place.

Reorder the number into 123.

3. Keep reordering.

Continue reordering the number you get.

What you get is still 123! So that is the number you will always get when you play with this kind of black hole numbers.
